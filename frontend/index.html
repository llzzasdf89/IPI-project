IPI-pen
For mental,anions and neutral molecules in rhizon
Produce number:pen100,pen200
Price:RMB 50
Construction:
The sampling zone of IPI-pen consists of the below fiber membrane tube,which is based on passive diffusion mechanism.Porewater is collected via the pipes connecting to double sides of the hollow fiber membrane tube.
Product specification:
IPI-pen can be repeatedly used to sample metals,anions and neutral molecules from saturated soil.
Application methods:
Insert IPI-pen directly into saturated soil.Before sampling,inject O2-free ultra-pure water into IPI-pen, after 24h equilibrium, the porewater can be sampled
Storage:
IPI-pen can be preserved in dark and dry condition for one year.
Notice:
Before deployment, IPI-pen should be soaked into O2-free ultra pure water for at least 1 hour to reduce the interference to soil sample.

For mental,anions and neutral molecules in rhizon
Produce number:ring400
Price:RMB 100
Construction:
IPI-ring has a diameter of 4 cm. The sampling zone of IPI-ring consists of the hollow fiber membrane tube, which is based on passive diffusion mechanism. Porewater is collected via the pipes connecting to double sides of the hollow fiber membrane tube.

IPI-profiler
IPI profiler for metals, anions and neutral molecules in soil-water interface at high-resolution
Produce number：profiler60
Price：RMB 800
Construction:
IPI profiler has a sampling depth of 60 mm, with a spatial resolution of 2 mm. The sampling zone of IPI profiler consists of an array of the hollow fiber membrane tubes, which is based on passive diffusion mechanism. Porewater is collected via the pipes connecting to double sides of the hollow fiber membrane tube.
Product specification:
IPI profiler can be repeatedly used to sample metals, anions and neutral molecules around soil-water interface at high-resolution.
Application methods:
Deploy root bag of wetland plants into IPI-ring. The system is buried in soils. Before sampling, inject O2-free ultra-pure water into IPI-ring, after 24h equilibrium, the porewater can be sampled.
Storage:
IPI profiler can be preserved in dark and dry condition for one year.
Notice:
Before deployment, IPI-ring should be soaked into O2-free ultra pure water for at least 1 hour to reduce the interference to soil sample.
